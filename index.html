⛑️ Common Questions

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS.

Everything you need to know before applying.

Lead agencies sell you contact information - a name and a phone number from someone who filled out a form online. You still have to call them, qualify them, and deal with the ones who aren't serious. Val Systems handles all of that. We only put people on your calendar after they've been verified for location, budget, timeline, and genuine intent. You skip straight to the appointment. No chasing. No filtering yourself.

After a homeowner responds to one of our ads, our team reaches out to verify key details: Are they in your service area? Do they have a realistic budget for the type of work you do? Do they have a timeline in mind? Are they the decision-maker? Only homeowners who pass every check get moved forward. The ones who don't make the cut never reach your calendar - period.

The volume depends on your market, your service area, and the type of work you do. We set realistic expectations upfront based on your specific situation - we don't promise numbers we can't deliver. What we can promise is that every appointment that lands on your calendar has been properly qualified. Quality over quantity, always.

Very little. We start with a strategy call to understand your business, your service area, your ideal client, and what "qualified" means for you specifically. From there, we set up the ad campaigns and the filtering workflow - you don't need to do anything technical. You need an active calendar link (like Calendly) and a working phone. That's it.

You can cancel anytime with no penalties, no fees, and no drama. We don't believe in trapping people in contracts. If the system isn't working for you - or if your situation changes - you're free to walk away. We'd rather you stay because the results are there, not because you're locked in.
