Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Val Systems is operated by Valery Media Group LLC.

Reservation Fee

To temporarily reserve a service territory and evaluate contractor eligibility, Val Systems charges a $25 reservation fee.

When the Fee Is Refundable

The $25 reservation fee will be fully refunded if:

  • The contractor is determined not to be a fit for the program
  • Val Systems declines the application
  • The requested territory is unavailable
  • Val Systems chooses not to proceed with onboarding

Approved refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

When the Fee Is Non-Refundable

The reservation fee becomes non-refundable once service delivery has started. Service delivery may include:

  • Territory approval
  • Onboarding
  • Campaign preparation
  • Lead generation setup
  • Appointment-setting activities
  • Advertising launch preparation

Processing Time

Approved refunds are generally processed within five (5) business days. Actual posting times depend on Stripe, banking institutions, and payment processors.

Chargebacks

If you believe a charge was made in error, please contact us before initiating a chargeback so we can attempt to resolve the matter.
